ED0C: command devices on the serial bus to LISTEN
Used by the routines at F250, F3D5, F5ED and FFB1.
ED0C 09 20 ORA #$20 OR with the LISTEN command
ED0E 20 A4 F0 JSR $F0A4 check RS232 bus idle
