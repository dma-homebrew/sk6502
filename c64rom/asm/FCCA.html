FCCA: stop the cassette motor
Used by the routines at FBCD and FC93.
FCCA A5 01 LDA $01 read the 6510 I/O port
FCCC 09 20 ORA #%00100000 mask xx1x xxxx, turn the cassette motor off
FCCE 85 01 STA $01 save the 6510 I/O port
FCD0 60 RTS
